# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 9, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Echo Mobile ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and associated services (collectively, the "Application"). This Application facilitates cryptocurrency token distribution, wallet connections, and blockchain interactions.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Blockchain and Wallet Information

- **Wallet Addresses**: Public blockchain addresses when you connect your cryptocurrency wallet
- **Transaction Data**: On-chain transaction records, token balances, and blockchain interactions
- **Smart Contract Interactions**: Records of your interactions with our token distribution smart contracts
- **Token Receipts**: Information about tokens and rewards distributed to your wallet

### 2. Device and Usage Information

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **NFC Data**: When using NFC handshake features, we process NFC tag data
- **Usage Analytics**: App usage patterns, features accessed, session duration
- **Log Data**: IP addresses, access times, app crashes, and performance data

### 3. Social Media Integration

- **X (Twitter) Integration**: When you connect your X account, we may access:
  - Public profile information
  - Authorization tokens for banner updates
  - Tweet posting capabilities (only when explicitly authorized)

### 4. Authentication Data

- **Device Verification**: Biometric data processed locally on your device for authentication
- **OAuth Tokens**: Temporary authentication tokens for third-party service integration

## Data Storage and Security

### Blockchain Data

Transaction data recorded on public blockchains (such as Solana) is permanently stored on the blockchain and is publicly accessible by nature. We do not control or have the ability to delete blockchain data.

### Off-Chain Data

Data stored on our servers is protected using industry-standard security measures including:

- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Access controls and authentication systems
- Regular security audits and monitoring

### Local Device Storage

Certain data, including authentication credentials and preferences, may be stored locally on your device. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

## Cryptocurrency-Specific Privacy Considerations

### Pseudonymity

Blockchain transactions are pseudonymous. While wallet addresses don't directly reveal your identity, transaction patterns can potentially be analyzed to link addresses to individuals.

### Public Blockchain Data

All blockchain transactions are publicly visible and permanently recorded. This includes:

- Token transfers
- Smart contract interactions
- Wallet balances
- Transaction timestamps

### Third-Party Block Explorers

Your transaction data may be accessible through third-party blockchain explorers and analytics platforms beyond our control.
